SecurITeam Wargame 2019

Welcome every hacker at this year's Wargame!

The second stage is unlocked by solving 2 challenges on the first stage. The solution of every assignment is a 32 characters long MD5 hash wrapped into "SECURITEAM{...}" (e.g. "SECURITEAM{0123456789abcdef0123456789abcdef}"). If you find the hash without the pre- and postfixes, then you should wrap it in yourself.

The hint button gives you some help but will halve the score of the challenge.

To reach the *.wargame sites (if there is any), see the instructions in the VPN menu.
